5. Explain, in a couple of paragraphs, what you think are two major challenges around DNS configuration for less-technical customers hosting websites.

     

I think the first major challenge around DNS configuration for less-technical customers would be understanding the what, when, and how. What record to use, when to use it, and how it works. Incorrect record entries or even conflicting configurations can cause issues.

The second challenge is the unfamiliarity of the proper steps/order in configuring the DNS. This could lead to SSL certificate validation issues. I learned that DNS records should be configured first, like adding the CNAME record before mapping the domain. Otherwise, it may prevent the validatin of the SSL certificate and it could result in a certificate name mismatch error or web browsers often display a warning such as "your connection is not private". Aside from that, there is no immediate feedback during the propagation, which means it can lead to uncertainty whether the issue is resolved after trying to reconfigure the DNS records

7. How would you set up an http 301 status redirect from “/netlify/anything” on your site, to https://www.google.com/search?q=anything. Please provide the redirect formatting here. Now, how about a proxy redirect? Please add that proxy redirect rule directly to your site. (Please add the proxy redirect to your website!)

For 301 Redirect:

from = "/netlify/anything"
to = "https://www.google.com/search?q=anything"
status = 301

As for Proxy Redirect, I've added the following rule directly to my site:

9. We understand you don't know anything about our internal procedures at this stage, but we want you to explain at a high level how you'd react to this situation: You receive a report of a severe security issue on www.netlify.com. You can't immediately confirm the report, so what steps might you take to investigate or substantiate the report? What might you say to the reporter, even though we haven't confirmed their assertion yet, that will instill confidence that our business is very concerned about security? You believe there is a reasonable chance the report is correct and the problem is very large and impactful. How might you escalate?

   

Upon receiving a report of a severe security issue on www.netlify.com, I would immediately launch an internal investigation. My first step would be to review our security logs for any signs of unusual activity such as unauthorized access attempts, abnormal traffic patterns, or unexpected configuration changes, and to check our monitoring tools for any related alerts. If the site relies on third-party plugins, I would also verify their security status and look for any known vulnerabilities. I would promptly acknowledge the reporter’s concern, thanking them for their vigilance, and assure them that we take security very seriously and are actively working to verify the issue.

If my initial investigation indicates that the threat is significant, I would consult with my team to see if similar issues have been reported before, and then escalate the matter to our senior security team immediately. This escalation would involve sharing all relevant details such as the evidence gathered, the potential scope and impact of the issue, and any recommended steps for further investigation or immediate action. Throughout the process, I would communicate transparently where appropriate, provide updates to the reporter to demonstrate our commitment to maintaining a secure platform while safeguarding all sensitive information.
